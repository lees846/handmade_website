A-Wake

Are you awake? Are you sure? If you’re awake, is that what makes you alive? The thesaurus seems to think so. Then why are “wakes” about someone who’s dead?

Or the wake of a boat or a duck; the wake is the water left behind, swirling and dodging the event which disturbed it. Is that how we live when we’re a-wake? Are we the wake? Do we watch the dead and dance in the undertow of the things we can’t control?

Who gets to be the boat?
