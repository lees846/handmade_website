Contradiction

One definition of “asleep” is “into the state of death.” What dies while you sleep? More importantly, what lives or is born anew? I think (can I think when I’m asleep?), I think dreams can make you alive, just as awake can. Not comatose, not slumbering, not languid, not dead said in a “nice way.” No. In my dreams, I feel alive. I know I’m alive.

Who can live without sleep anyways?
