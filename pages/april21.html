A Dream from April 21

I had this crazy dream once where I was getting my wisdom teeth removed. You may guess that with a dream like this, something would go horribly wrong at the dentist, but the surgery seemed to go smoothly. I was home with my mom and my brother after the fact, ready to be eating soup and mac n cheese for a week, and we all began looking at our phones. Perhaps there was a notification, or maybe we just knew to check. Regardless, on Twitter was a note from our Governor:

“Today we extend the closure of K-12 schools... through the end of the school year.”

Was I wrong? Sports, prom, graduation... had those been merely dreams?

I can't seem to wake up from this one. Even 2 years later.
